## About This Program

This repository hosts the academic research program around the VAIOS protocol — an open protocol for AI-native organizational intelligence ([spec](https://github.com/fabio-dorano/vaios)).

The research program investigates governance architectures for organizations where AI operates as a governed agent with functional hierarchy, rather than as a tool or object of governance. Concepts originate from applied research: the VAIOS protocol was implemented before it was formalized, and the research program documents, formalizes, and extends what was discovered empirically.

Dorano is the organization where VAIOS is developed and applied. In academic publications, VAIOS is the object of study and Dorano is the case study organization.

## AI Disclosure

The VAIOS system was developed through iterative co-creation sessions between the author and AI systems (Claude, Anthropic), operating as governed agents within the VAIOS protocol itself. AI systems contributed to technical analysis, formalization, and structured documentation under human direction, review, and final approval.

All intellectual contributions, conceptual innovations, and research direction are the responsibility of the human author. AI disclosure in publications follows current COPE, Elsevier, Springer Nature, and ACM guidelines.
